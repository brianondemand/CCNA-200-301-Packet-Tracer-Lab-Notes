### Syslog — CCNA Quick Notes

- **Syslog** is used to **collect and store log messages** from network devices.

- Helps administrators **monitor, troubleshoot, and audit** networks.

- Devices such as routers, switches, firewalls, and servers can send logs to a **Syslog Server**.

**Port:**

- UDP 514



**Severity Levels (0–7):**


0 - Emergency
1 - Alert
2 - Critical
3 - Error
4 - Warning
5 - Notification
6 - Informational
7 - Debugging


**Example:**

Router → Syslog Server
       "Interface Gig0/0 changed state to down"


**Remember:**

- **SNMP = Monitor devices**
- **Syslog = Record device events and errors**
- **NTP = Synchronize time**
