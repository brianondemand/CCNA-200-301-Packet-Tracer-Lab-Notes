# Voice VLANs – CCNA Complete Summary Notes

## What is a Voice VLAN?

* A **Voice VLAN** is a separate VLAN used specifically for **IP phones**.
* It separates **voice traffic** from **data traffic** on the same switch port.
* Improves voice quality, security, and network management.

---

## Why Use a Voice VLAN?

* Prioritizes voice traffic.
* Reduces delays (latency) and jitter.
* Separates voice devices from PCs.
* Makes troubleshooting easier.

---

## Typical Setup

```text
PC
 │
 ▼
IP Phone
 │
 ▼
Switch
```

The phone and PC share the same switch port:

* PC traffic → Data VLAN
* Phone traffic → Voice VLAN

Example:

```text
Data VLAN  = VLAN 10
Voice VLAN = VLAN 20
```

---

## How It Works

An IP phone tags its voice traffic with the Voice VLAN ID.

Example:

```text
PC Traffic     → VLAN 10
Phone Traffic  → VLAN 20
```

The switch can distinguish between voice and data traffic even though both use the same physical port.

---

## Benefits

* Better call quality.
* Easier management.
* Increased security.
* Supports QoS (Quality of Service).

---

## Cisco Configuration Example

Assign Data VLAN:

```cisco
switchport access vlan 10
```

Assign Voice VLAN:

```cisco
switchport voice vlan 20
```

Complete example:

```cisco
interface fa0/1
 switchport mode access
 switchport access vlan 10
 switchport voice vlan 20
```

---

## Voice VLAN and QoS

Voice traffic is sensitive to:

* Delay (Latency)
* Jitter
* Packet Loss

Voice VLANs are commonly used with **QoS** to prioritize voice packets over normal data traffic.

---

## Traffic Example

```text
PC → VLAN 10 → Normal Data

IP Phone → VLAN 20 → Voice Traffic
```

---

## Verification Commands

Show VLAN information:

```cisco
show vlan brief
```

Show interface switchport details:

```cisco
show interfaces switchport
```

---

## CCNA Exam Tips

### Key Commands

```cisco
switchport access vlan 10
switchport voice vlan 20
```

### Remember

* One switch port can carry:

  * Data VLAN traffic
  * Voice VLAN traffic
* IP phones typically use a dedicated Voice VLAN.
* Voice VLANs improve voice quality and support QoS.

---

## Common Exam Question

**Can a switch port belong to both a Data VLAN and a Voice VLAN?**

**Yes.**

Example:

```text
Data VLAN  = 10
Voice VLAN = 20
```

The PC uses VLAN 10, while the IP phone uses VLAN 20 on the same switch port.
